How it works

Built for how
real people actually work

Designed from the ground up to be offline-first, instantly responsive, and genuinely satisfying to use every day.

Step 01
Add your tasks & habits
Create tasks with priorities, due dates, and icons. Set up recurring habits with exact reminder times — daily, weekly, or on a custom interval.
Step 02
Get notified at the right time
Android native notifications fire at exactly the time you set — even in Doze mode, even after a reboot. Task due reminders batch at 9 AM automatically.
Step 03
Check off your day
Tap the Today page each morning to see your habits and tasks for the day. Lock future dates, review past completions, and maintain your streaks.
Step 04
Earn rewards as you grow
Unlock badges and new accent colors as you hit streak milestones. The app gamifies consistency without feeling gimmicky or distracting.
